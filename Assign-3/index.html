MELTING HEARTS, CHOCOLATE ART.

Ferrero Rocher, a delectable confection, is renowned for its luxurious combination of a whole roasted hazelnut encased in a thin wafer shell filled with velvety hazelnut cream and coated in smooth milk chocolate and crunchy hazelnut pieces. Each golden sphere offers a perfect blend of textures and flavors, creating an exquisite experience that delights chocolate enthusiasts worldwide.

WHY CHOOSE FERRERO ROCHER?

  • 5.5 grams of total fat, with around 2 grams being saturated fat.
  • No cholesterol.
  • Approximately 6.1 grams of total carbohydrates, including around 0.5 grams of dietary fiber and 5.5 grams of sugars.
  • Around 0.9 grams of protein
